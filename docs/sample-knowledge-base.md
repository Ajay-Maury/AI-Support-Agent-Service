# Siddharth Traders — Customer Support Knowledge Base

## 1. Return & Refund Policy

Customers may return products within 7 days of delivery if the items are unused, in their original packaging, and accompanied by the original invoice. Perishable goods such as whole spices with a shelf life under 30 days are not eligible for return.

To initiate a return, contact our support team at support@siddharthtraders.in with your Order ID and reason for return. Refunds are processed within 5–7 working days after the returned item is received and inspected at our warehouse.

Damaged or incorrect items must be reported within 48 hours of delivery with photographic evidence. In such cases, a full refund or replacement will be issued at no additional cost.

---

## 2. Order Placement & Minimum Order Quantity

Retailers can place orders through the Siddharth Traders mobile app or web portal. The minimum order quantity (MOQ) for most SKUs is 5 kg for dry fruits and 2 kg for whole spices. Blended masala products have an MOQ of 1 kg.

Bulk orders above 50 kg qualify for tiered discounts: 5% off for 50–100 kg, 8% off for 100–200 kg, and 12% off for orders exceeding 200 kg. Discounts are applied automatically at checkout.

Custom blending orders require a minimum of 25 kg per blend and a lead time of 3–5 working days.

---

## 3. Delivery & Shipping

Standard delivery is available across all Tier-1 and Tier-2 cities in India, with an estimated delivery time of 2–4 working days. Tier-3 cities and rural areas may experience delivery times of 5–7 working days.

Free shipping is available on orders above ₹5,000. Orders below this threshold attract a flat shipping fee of ₹150.

Express delivery (next-day) is available in Delhi NCR, Mumbai, Bengaluru, and Hyderabad for orders placed before 12:00 PM IST. Express delivery charges are ₹250 flat.

Cold-chain delivery for premium dry fruits (e.g., Kashmiri saffron, pistachios) is available on request at an additional charge of ₹100 per shipment.

---

## 4. Payment Methods

We accept the following payment methods:
- UPI (GPay, PhonePe, Paytm, BHIM)
- Net Banking (all major banks)
- Credit / Debit Cards (Visa, Mastercard, RuPay)
- NEFT / RTGS for bulk orders above ₹1,00,000
- Credit terms of Net-30 available for registered distributors with a minimum 6-month trading history

Cash on Delivery (COD) is available for orders up to ₹10,000 in select pin codes.

---

## 5. Product Quality & Certifications

All products are sourced directly from APMC-registered farmers and mandis. Siddharth Traders holds FSSAI License No. 10019022004152 and follows ISO 22000:2018 food safety standards.

Each batch undergoes in-house quality testing for moisture content, foreign matter, and microbial load before dispatch. Certificates of Analysis (CoA) are available on request for bulk orders.

Our premium range of dry fruits (almonds, cashews, walnuts) is sourced from certified farms in California, Chile, and Kashmir respectively.

---

## 6. Account & Registration

New retailers can register on the platform by providing their GSTIN, business PAN, and a valid trade license. Verification is typically completed within 24 hours on working days.

Distributors must submit additional KYC documents including a cancelled cheque and address proof. Distributor accounts receive access to wholesale pricing tiers not visible to retail accounts.

Forgot your password? Use the "Forgot Password" option on the login screen. An OTP will be sent to your registered mobile number and email address.

---

## 7. Cancellation Policy

Orders can be cancelled free of charge within 2 hours of placement. After 2 hours, cancellation is subject to a 2% processing fee if the order has not yet been dispatched.

Once an order is marked "Dispatched", cancellation is not possible. Please refer to the Return Policy for post-delivery options.

Subscription orders (auto-reorder) can be paused or cancelled anytime from the "My Subscriptions" section in the app.
